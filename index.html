Checking your grades should be easy.

View your grades with a functional, beautiful interface on Android, iOS, or Chrome. Get notified when your grade changes in a class.

Checking your grades should be free.

manaTEAMS is completely free and completely open source. We will never charge or show ads for a service we feel deserves to be available to all.

Checking your grades should be secure.

All scraping is done on the client side, meaning that it's impossible for us to see your grades. The project is also open source, so anyone can take a glance at our code.
